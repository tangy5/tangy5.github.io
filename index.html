I am currently a second year graduate student at Department of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering of Vanderbilt University. My supervisor is Professor Bennett A. Landman. Our Medical-image Analysis and Statistical Interpretation lab (MASI Lab) performs translational research to explore innovative, clinically useful techniques. I work on a wide range of challenges in statistical machine learning (mathematics-large scale datasets-computer vision) and am especially interested in application of medical image analysis and computational biomedicine. I received a M.S. from NYU in 2018 and was a reaserach intern of Siemens Healthineers (Princeton, New Jersey). Before this, I complete my B.S. at Tianjin University. I joined Vanderbilt Institute of Surgery Engineering as a graduate research/teaching assistant. I served as lecturer in the department of computer science teaching undergraduate courses.
